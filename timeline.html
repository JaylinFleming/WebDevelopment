TIMELINE

1995

3dfx Voodoo Graphics

The Voodoo card marked the first major leap in consumer 3D acceleration and popularized dedicated graphics add-in boards.

1999

NVIDIA GeForce 256

Coined the term “GPU” and brought hardware transform & lighting, reshaping real-time 3D rendering.

2006

CUDA

Enabled general-purpose compute on GPUs, paving the way for acceleration in AI, simulation, and data analytics.

2010's

Rise of GPU

GPUs moved beyond graphics into scientific research, crypto mining, and large-scale machine learning.

2020's

AI Era

Modern GPUs (e.g., RTX & RDNA families) power gaming realism and the training/inference of cutting-edge AI systems.
